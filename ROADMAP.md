# Roadmap

## v0.1.x Alpha

- Stabilize image-to-STL geometry quality.
- Improve vector cleanup, detail classification, and review previews.
- Keep CLI and PySide6 GUI workflows working from the same pipeline.

## Upcoming

- Better STL mesh generation from true vector paths.
- Mesh repair and manifold validation.
- Product presets for keychains, wall art, charms, and layered color relief.
- Queue persistence and job history.
- Dashboard/database support.
- Bambu Studio, Printables, Creality, and MakerWorld integrations.

## Later

- AI image generation.
- Blender automation.
- Automatic product descriptions and upload packages.
